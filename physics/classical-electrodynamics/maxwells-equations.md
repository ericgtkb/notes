# Maxwell's Equations

Maxwell's equations in vacuum are (in Gaussian units)

$$
\begin{align}
&\div\mb{E}=4\pi\rho,\\
&\div\mb{B}=0,\\
&\curl\mb{E}+\frac{1}{c}\pdv{\mb{B}}{t}=0,\\
&\curl\mb{B}-\frac{1}{c}\pdv{\mb{E}}{t}=\frac{4\pi}{c}\mb{J}.
\end{align}
$$
